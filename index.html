Brand Strategy

Lolly Pop defined her values as natural, simple, and fun. I approached each value, researched it, and focused on the word "natural" to refine it in a deep sense.
I realized the importance of taking responsibility and moving towards a more harmonious relationship with nature.
For the design concept, I chose to focus on the common good, a shared set of values that give space and respect to natural ingredients. I presented the process that the sweets go through so that everyone can be a part of it. The branding work was designed to influence and convey the passion and purpose of Lolly Pop.

I created a quiet yet expressive tone that can take people on a full tour around the candy world of Lolly Pop.

Logo

The logo design was inspired by the brand words natural, simple, and fun I wanted to capture the nostalgic joy of enjoying candy as a child, so I incorporated a simple illustration of a candy to evoke a sense of playfulness. The blue color and block alignment were chosen to convey our brand's meaningful message and emphasize the importance of healthy ingredients. Our logo aims to evoke positive emotions and create a fun and memorable brand identity.

Visual Language

I found inspiration in the tranquil and elegant fields of the Land of Israel . Using illustrations of the actual fruit sources and drawing from the beauty of flowers, I aimed to capture their essence in a simple yet captivating way. I wanted to exude confidence without being overly formal, so I opted for light and stable illustrations that convey a clear purpose with a touch of playfulness. To emphasize the organic and handmade feel, I chose simple vector illustrations and a raw, authentic atmosphere. For the font, I selected Robinson, a delicate typeface with a hint of calligraphy that adds a human touch and blends harmoniously with the natural flow.
